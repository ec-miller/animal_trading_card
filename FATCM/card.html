Platypus

Did you know that the Platypus has poisonous venum?

  • Scientific Name: Ornithorhynchus anatinus
  • Average Length: 50 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 17 years
  • Habitat: Small streams and rivers

Duck-Billed Platypuses are native to Australia and are mostly nocturnal creatures. Water is the platypus' favorite place to be. They are one of two mammals that lay eggs!
